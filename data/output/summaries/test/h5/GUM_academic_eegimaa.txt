This paper presents the location of the Gújjolaay Eegimaa language, a Jóola language spoken in the Basse Casamance area of Southern Senegal, mainly in Mof-Ávvi, with an estimated population of 7000-11,200 speakers, closely related languages like Kujireray/Kuluunaay and Gufiñamay, and that speakers often live in urban areas like Ziguinchor and Dakar.
