Vava'u is a group of more than 50 islands in Tonga, which was inhabited by early Polynesians approximately 3000 years ago, has an official port of entry for over 500 yachts every sailing season called Neiafu, has a tropical climate with average temperatures from 24 to 29º C, and has three main islands: Utu Vava’u, Pangaimotu, and Hunga Island.
